Bayesian ad attribution: seven-scenario explorer

Seven synthetic campaigns run through the same Bayesian structural time-series forecasting counterfactual. Each tab shows an honest plain-language verdict, the forecast with its credible interval and attributed area, the weekly and total numbers, and the Bayesian diagnostics behind them.

Method: Bayesian structural time-series counterfactual (PyMC), donors as regressors with a local level and weekly seasonality. MCMC seeded, 2 chains, 800 draws. Data files under docs/data/.
