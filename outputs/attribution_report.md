## Donor selection and the fanbase pitfall

From 30 candidates, 19 were selected (19 genuinely similar, 0 unrelated). Minimum pre-period level correlation among selected donors: 0.768.
- The same-artist other-region (fanbase) series has pre-period level correlation 0.309 and daily-change correlation 0.068 with the target audience. It is flat and correctly excluded.

## Annual / day-of-year component inclusion rule

The annual Fourier component is added only when the pre-period spans at least 365 days. Growth and decline scenarios have a 120-day pre-period, so the annual component is correctly OMITTED (False). The long-history scenario has a 430-day pre-period, so the annual component is INCLUDED (True).
- Long-history recovery: estimated cumulative effect 169,756 vs true 173,683 (2.3% error), true effect covered: True.

## Credible-interval coverage over 40 simulated campaigns

Each campaign has a known injected effect. We check how often the 95% credible interval contains the true cumulative effect (calibration) and how well the point estimate recovers it (accuracy).

| model | 95% CI coverage | median abs error (cumulative) | mean rel-lift error | mean CI width |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| seasonality-aware | 100% | 3.4% | 1.2% | 35,474 |
| seasonality-blind | 100% | 4.6% | 1.4% | 52,960 |

- The seasonality-aware model recovers the relative lift to within 1.2% on average with 95% credible-interval coverage of 100% (near the nominal 95%).
- The seasonality-blind model must inflate its observation noise to absorb the unmodeled weekly pattern, so its intervals are about 49% wider (100% coverage, over the nominal 95%) with no gain in accuracy. It is the less sharp and less well-calibrated model.
- Across all coverage fits, worst R-hat 1.090 and 110 total divergences.

## Multi-region hierarchical reconciliation (Bayesian + MinT)

Each region and the national aggregate get their own Bayesian counterfactual. Independent estimates are incoherent; MinT reconciliation (weighted by the posterior variance of each node) makes them coherent and reduces error.

### Illustrative campaign (fixed seed)

- Aggregate estimate: 217,768. Sum of independent per-region estimates: 207,050. Incoherence gap: -10,718.
- True total injected effect: 211,953.

| method | total estimate | error vs true total |
|---|---|---|
| bottom-up | 207,050 | -4,904 |
| aggregate-only | 217,768 | +5,814 |
| reconciled (MinT) | 209,606 | -2,348 |
